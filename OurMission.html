Our Mission:

Doa is designed to be a unique D-10 table-top role playing game that strays from the timeworn path of more established RPGs and creates a streamlined environment conducive to any game setting the players desire. The game must hold true to this:

1. It must be “simple”.

2. It must use “elegant mathematics”

3. It must have far reaching concepts such as worldwide intrigue, famine or cultural shifts.

4. Unique flora and fauna (original within Doa itself)

5. It must be comprehensible to the novice gamer

6. Cohesion at all levels of the game rules and the lore must be present.

7. The mechanics of the game and the lore itself must add to the suspension of disbelief for the enjoyment of children and adults alike!
